Millennial Conference on Number Theory

May 21 - 26, 2000

University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

The "Millennial Conference on Number Theory" is an international conference held at the conclusion of the Special Year in Number Theory 1999/2000 at the University of Illinois.

  • Scope: The conference will be open to all areas of number theory.

  • Venue: The conference will be held on the campus of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Hotel and dormitory accommodation is available within easy walking distance of the conference site.

  • Plenary Talks: Confirmed plenary speakers include:

  • Contributed Talks: We expect to be able to accommodate about 100 short talks; details will be announced at a later point.

  • Conference Proceedings: The proceedings of the conference will be published.

  • Additional Information: More information will be posted on this web page (http://www.math.uiuc.edu/nt2000/millennial.html) as it becomes available.
