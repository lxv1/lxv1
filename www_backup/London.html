Theatre:
Stand up just like the   sixteenth century   'groundlings' used to do at Shakespeare's Globe  Theatre for only £5.
 Some fringe theatres   such as Tricycle Theatre   have 'pay as much as you  can afford' performances   on Monday nights.
    The Lyric Hammersmith,  Royal Court Theatre   Downstairs and Soho   Theatre have all seats
 at £5 on Mondays. The      Orange Tree Theatre in     Richmond has £5 tickets
    for Wednesday matinees,  the Barbican has bench  tickets for £5 on Saturday afternoons and     the Soho Theatre has     lunchtime theatre Monday to Saturday for just £3.
     The Royal Court Theatre  Downstairs even has a    few standing tickets at  10p!
 Fringe theatre tickets   on most days start at   about £6 while West End  ticket prices start at   about £8-£10,
but some   shows such as the   Complete Works of     William Shakespeare      (abridged) start at £8.
The Half Price Ticket   Booth in Leicester   Square will help you get  cheaper tickets for the  big productions.
