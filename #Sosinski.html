From a student work at Russian mathematical olympiad, about 1962:


Пришел сюда с большой надеждой

Прочел задач и сказалЬ

Какой безграмотный невежда

Ции задачи сочинял?

…….


(If the Russian text is not readable, choose edit page from file menu.)
 

Translation by A.Sosinski:


I came here eager, full of hope
Then I read the problems and declared:
What academic stuffy dope
These crummy problems has prepared?
