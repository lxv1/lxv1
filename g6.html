Example of Roullette

Coupon: Face value $5. Use at least $5 of your money with coupon. Coupon is not returned. What is its value?

Solution:
Recall that the value is -1/19 of your bet  (apr. -5%,  with 00).  For best value use $5 of your money. The payoff:
(18/19)*5 (from coupon) - (1/19)*5 (from your $5) =(17/19)*5  ($) (appr.  $4.47).

I used 4 coupons (from 2 fun books in Cains), so expected payoff was  about $17.89. Factual payoff was  $20 (Aussi dolars)
