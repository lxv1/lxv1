PICTURES FROM THE EXPO, April 1-30, 2000,  Shakespear's Sister,  S Pugh, State College, PA
Anna received her Bachelor of Arts degree from the St. John's College in Annapolis, MD, in 1993.
Her work is the closest in style to the school of German expressionism, which sought to convey
the subjective emotions of the artist rather than to portray objective reality.
