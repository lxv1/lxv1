NASA Program Assistant

Are you interested in working with a NASA organization?   Do you have 
experience with Microsoft Word, Excel and Access and HTML?  The 
Pennsylvania Space Grant Consortium will be interviewing for a Student 
Program Assistant who can assist with NASA Sponsored Activities (such as 
WISER, MURE, Space Day and Teacher Workshops).  This could include 
developing/maintaining databases, developing/revising flyers/brochures, 
assist coordination of events and provide backup for general office 
support.  The qualified student should be available to work 10-20 hours 
during the Spring 2005 Semester (and possibly Summer) and have experience 
with Microsoft software programs and html programming.

WORK STUDY students are encouraged to apply.  To find out if you qualify 
for WORK STUDY, please check your eLion account or the Office of Student Aid.
