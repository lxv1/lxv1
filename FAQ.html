division by 0


Q. Why division by 0 is not allowed?

A.  Division by 0 in the correct statement 0∙0 =  0∙1  produces the false statement  0 = 1.

Q. I missed the test, because  it was before I spoke with my advisor (changed my major, paid my tuition, arrived to the country). Why I cannot have a make-up test?

A. You did not write me before missing class or you gave me a false reason. You are subject to penalty for false reasons.

homework



Q. When exactly  the first homework is due? 

Do it before the first class on Monday. Late homework  penalty starts after the due date, i.e., after 11:59:59p  on W as recorded by Angel computer.


Q. In my homework I used a computer (a website,…) to get my answer but you gave me no credit. Why?

A. You get no credit for a wrong answer no matter how you got it.

Even if your answer is right, you may get no points if it is not clear how you got it.

If you use a computer, write exactly what hardware and software you used. 

If you used a website, write the URL.


Q. I was late with homework because I did not get the textbook on time. Can you cancel the late homework penalty.

No,  I cannot. The textbook is required.

I do not give or sell the textbook. There is a copy on reserve in the library.

The basic facts can be found in Handbook of Linear Algebra and other ebooks and books in the library. But only our textbook has questions for homework.


Q. Where  can I find the questions for homework?

A. Unless said otherwise, they are excercices in the textbook.





midterms


Q. What if I required to solve  an optimization problem in a test which have more than one optimal solution?

A. If you are not required to find more than one optimal solution, one is sufficient for full credit.

You may get bonus points for finding more optimal solutions.


Q. What if in a multiple-choice test with scantron, none of suggested answers is correct?

A. Write this.  Read the instruction to the test about what to choose at scantron (usually, E).


Q. What if in a multiple-choice test with scantrons, there is more than one correct answer?

A. In your solution, point out all correct answers. On scantron, choose one correct answer.


Q. What if in a multiple-choice test with scantrons, there are no no correct answer?

A. In your solution, write down that all answers are wrong and explain  why.

On scantron, choose E





class presemtation


Q. Why I got too few points for my class presentation?

A. Maybe, you did not tell anything new, or anything correct, or did not give proper references.

Maybe, whatever  was new and correct was not related with linear programming.

Maybe, there was not enough time for your presentation.



Q. In my class presentation I used what I learned from web (newspaper, another class,…), but you did not give me any credit. Why? 

A. You get no credit for wrong statements no matter how you learned them.



Q. I spend 6 hours preparing my class presentation but got no points. Why?

The amount of time you spent for a class presentation is not graded.
