From a student work at Russian mathematical olimpiad, about 1962:

Пришел  сюда с болшой  надеждой
Прочел  задачи и сказал:
Kакой   безграмотный  невежда
Cии  задачи  сочинял?

(If the Russian text is not readible, choose edit page from file menu.)
 

Translation by A.Sosinski:


I came here eager, full of hope
Then I read the problems and declared:
What academic stuffy dope
These crummy problems has prepared?
